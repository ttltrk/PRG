FOR LOOPS

---

```python
sqr = ["red", "yellow", "green", "purple", "blue"]

for i in range(0,5):
  sqr[i] = "white"

print(sqr)
  
>>>
['white', 'white', 'white', 'white', 'white']
>>>
```
