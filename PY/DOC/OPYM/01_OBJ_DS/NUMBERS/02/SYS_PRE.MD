### System Prefix

---

|Number|System Prefix|
|-----|------|
|Binary|'0b' or '0B'|
|Octal|'0o' or '0O'|
|Hexadecimal|'0x' or '0X'|

```python
print(0b1101011)
print(0xFB + 0b10)
print(0o15)

>>>
107
253
13
>>>
```
